Mawjat's visual arts section is dedicated to the full spectrum of contemporary visual expression — from painting and sculpture to large-scale installation and digital art. Our critics and contributors cover gallery openings, museum exhibitions, art fairs, and studio visits to bring you in-depth engagement with the visual world.

## Our Approach

Mawjat reviews visual art with seriousness and accessibility in mind. Our critics aim to situate works within their cultural and historical context, making criticism readable for both dedicated art-world insiders and curious newcomers.

We are particularly interested in work that emerges from or responds to cultures of the Arab world, the Francophone world, and the broader Global South — but our coverage is not limited to these geographies.

## Featured Themes

Recurring themes across our visual arts coverage include:

- Identity, diaspora, and belonging in contemporary art
- The intersection of traditional craft and contemporary practice
- Institutional critique and the politics of the art market
- Environmental and ecological art
- Archival and documentary practices
