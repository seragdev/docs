Mawjat treats photography as both an art form and a journalistic medium. Our photography section publishes portfolios, critical essays, exhibition reviews, and interviews with photographers whose work documents, interprets, and transforms the world around them.

## Photography Genres

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Documentary Photography" icon="camera">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Fine Art Photography" icon="image">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Street Photography" icon="city">

## Photo Essays

Mawjat publishes extended photo essays — sequences of images accompanied by text — that allow photographers to develop a visual narrative over multiple frames. Photo essays are among the most-read features in the magazine.

## Photographer Profiles

We regularly feature profiles of photographers at various career stages, from established figures with international recognition to emerging photographers whose work deserves wider attention.

## Exhibition Reviews

Photography exhibitions at galleries and museums receive the same critical attention we give to any visual art show. Our reviewers consider both the individual images and the curatorial framework that brings them together.

## Submit Photography Work

Mawjat accepts portfolio submissions, photo essay pitches, and photography criticism from contributors worldwide.
