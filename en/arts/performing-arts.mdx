The performing arts are among the most immediate and ephemeral of cultural forms — and Mawjat is committed to documenting and critiquing them with the attention they deserve. Our performing arts coverage spans theatre, contemporary dance, opera, circus arts, and interdisciplinary live performance.

## Reviews & Previews

Mawjat publishes reviews of productions from major festivals and venues, as well as previews of upcoming work. Our reviewers attend performances across the Arab world, Europe, and internationally.

Reviews are written to give readers a vivid sense of what they might have seen — or missed — while situating the work within broader artistic and cultural conversations.

## Festivals & Events

We provide dedicated coverage of major performing arts festivals, including international theatre festivals, dance biennials, and arts weeks that celebrate performance from across cultures.
