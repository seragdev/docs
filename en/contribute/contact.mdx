## Response Times

We do our best to respond to all messages. Please allow the following times before following up:

- **Editorial & submissions**: up to 4 weeks
- **Press enquiries**: up to 5 business days
- **Advertising & partnerships**: up to 10 business days
- **Reader feedback**: up to 2 weeks

<Note>
If you are pitching a contribution, please read our [submission guidelines](/en/contribute/submissions) first. Pitches sent without reviewing the guidelines may not receive a response.
</Note>

## Social Media

You can also follow and message Mawjat on social media. We are active on Instagram and X (formerly Twitter), where we share new content, editorial highlights, and cultural recommendations.
