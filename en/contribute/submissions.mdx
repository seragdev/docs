Mawjat welcomes contributions from writers, critics, artists, photographers, and translators worldwide. Whether you are an established cultural professional or an emerging voice, we encourage you to pitch your work to us.

## Who Can Submit

Mawjat accepts submissions from contributors of all backgrounds and career stages. We are particularly interested in voices that are underrepresented in mainstream cultural media — including writers from the Arab world, the Francophone world, and the Global South.

Contributions may be submitted in Arabic, French, or English.

## How to Pitch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read recent issues">
    Familiarise yourself with Mawjat's tone, style, and the types of pieces we publish before pitching. This will help you tailor your pitch appropriately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare your pitch">
    Write a concise pitch (200–300 words) that describes the piece you want to write, why it is relevant to Mawjat's readership, and why you are the right person to write it. Include a short biography (50–100 words).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your pitch">
    Email your pitch to the relevant section editor (see below). Include "Pitch: [Section] – [Working Title]" in the subject line.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for a response">
    Our editors aim to respond to all pitches within four weeks. If you have not heard back after four weeks, you may send a polite follow-up.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Submission Guidelines

<Accordion title="Word counts and lengths">
  - **Reviews**: 400–800 words
  - **Features & profiles**: 1,000–3,000 words
  - **Opinion & essays**: 800–2,500 words
  - **Photo essays**: 8–20 images with 200–500 words of accompanying text
  - **Poetry**: Up to 5 poems per submission
  - **Short fiction**: Up to 3,000 words
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Formatting requirements">
  Submit text as a Word document (.docx) or plain text file. Do not use complex formatting, headers, or tables. Photographs should be submitted as high-resolution JPEG or TIFF files (minimum 2,400px on the longest edge, 300dpi).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Rights and payment">
  Mawjat purchases first publication rights in the language of submission. Rights revert to the author/artist after publication. We pay contributors at our standard rates, which are communicated on acceptance. We do not accept speculative submissions of completed work for most sections — please pitch first.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Editorial process">
  All accepted work is subject to editorial review. Our editors will work with you to develop your piece before publication. Final editorial decisions rest with the editors.
</Accordion>
