Mawjat's opinion and essay section is a space for voices that have something urgent or challenging to say about culture and its contexts. We publish analytical essays, personal essays, cultural criticism, and polemics that contribute to the intellectual life of the magazine.

## What We Publish

## Editorial Standards

Opinion pieces in Mawjat are clearly labelled as opinion and represent the views of the author, not the editorial position of the magazine. We are committed to publishing a range of perspectives and to fostering constructive intellectual debate.

All opinion pieces are edited for clarity, coherence, and factual accuracy. We do not publish defamatory content.

## Length & Format

Opinion essays typically run between 800 and 2,500 words. We prefer pieces that develop a single clear argument rather than surveys of multiple topics.
