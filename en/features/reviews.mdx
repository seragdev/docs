## Our Critical Approach

Mawjat reviews are written with dual goals: to provide genuine critical assessment of a work's quality and achievement, and to situate the work within its cultural, historical, and artistic context. We do not publish promotional copy or press-release summaries — we publish criticism.

All reviewers are identified by name. We believe in transparent, accountable criticism.
