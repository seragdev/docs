Interviews are at the heart of Mawjat's editorial identity. We believe that the most valuable cultural journalism is built on genuine, in-depth conversation. Our interviews give artists and thinkers the space to speak at length about their work, their process, and the ideas that drive them.

## Interview Format

Mawjat interviews are long-form — typically between 1,500 and 4,000 words. We give our subjects the space to develop ideas in depth, and our interviewers bring rigorous preparation and genuine curiosity to every conversation.

Interviews are conducted in the subject's preferred language (Arabic, French, or English) and published in the relevant edition of the magazine. Selected interviews are translated and published across multiple editions.

## Archive

Mawjat maintains an archive of all published interviews. You can browse interviews by subject area using the Arts and Culture sections of each language edition.
