Mawjat is a trilingual art and culture magazine that bridges creative communities across the Arabic-speaking world, the Francophone world, and the English-speaking world. Each issue brings together criticism, interviews, essays, and reportage from artists, writers, and thinkers who are shaping contemporary culture.

## Our Three Languages

Mawjat publishes original content in three languages — Arabic, French, and English. Each language edition features unique content written by contributors who are native voices in their respective cultural contexts. Some pieces are translated across editions; others appear exclusively in one language.

<Note>
You can switch between language editions using the tabs at the top of the navigation sidebar.
</Note>

## Our Mission

Mawjat was founded on the belief that art and culture deserve serious, accessible, and multilingual coverage. We are committed to amplifying underrepresented artistic voices, supporting independent creators, and fostering dialogue between cultures through the shared language of art.

Whether you are a curious reader, a practising artist, or a cultural professional, Mawjat welcomes you.
