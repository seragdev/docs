Mawjat is brought to life by a diverse team of editors, writers, critics, photographers, and translators from across the Arab world, the Francophone world, and the English-speaking world. Our team combines deep cultural knowledge with a passion for rigorous, accessible journalism.

## Editorial Leadership

The magazine's editorial team oversees content across all three language editions. Our editors are practising critics and cultural professionals who bring firsthand expertise to every section they lead.

## Contributors

Mawjat publishes work by a wide network of freelance writers, critics, artists, and photographers. Contributors come from more than 20 countries and bring a range of cultural perspectives to the magazine.

If you are interested in becoming a contributor, please review our [submission guidelines](/en/contribute/submissions).

## Translators

Our translation team ensures that key pieces reach readers across all three language editions. Translators work closely with original authors to maintain voice and nuance across languages.

<Note>
Want to join the team? We welcome pitches from experienced arts and culture writers in all three languages. See our [submissions page](/en/contribute/submissions) for details.
</Note>
