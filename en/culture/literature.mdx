Mawjat's literature section is a space for serious engagement with the written word in all its forms — poetry, short fiction, novels, personal essays, and literary criticism. We review new publications, revisit classics, and spotlight writers whose work is reshaping literary culture.

## Multilingual Literature

Because Mawjat publishes in Arabic, French, and English, our literature section has a particular interest in works that cross linguistic boundaries — through translation, bilingual publication, or themes of linguistic identity. We review literature originally written in all three languages, as well as works translated into them.

## Book Reviews

Our book reviews aim to do more than summarise a plot. We situate each work within its literary and cultural context, consider its formal qualities, and assess its contribution to ongoing literary conversations.

## Author Spotlights

Mawjat publishes in-depth profiles of writers at all career stages — from debut authors to established figures. These profiles often include extracts from our published interviews.
