Mawjat's music section spans the full breadth of musical culture — from classical Arabic maqam and Western classical traditions, to jazz, electronic music, hip-hop, and experimental sound. We review albums, cover concerts and festivals, and publish in-depth interviews with musicians and composers.

## Album Reviews

Our album reviews go beyond describing what a record sounds like — we situate music within its cultural moment, consider its relationship to tradition and innovation, and assess its artistic achievement.

## Concert & Festival Coverage

Mawjat publishes concert reviews and festival reports from major music events. Our contributors attend live performances across the Arab world, North Africa, Europe, and internationally.

## Music Interviews

Conversations with musicians are a cornerstone of our music section. We speak with composers, performers, producers, and music educators about their practice, their influences, and the cultural contexts in which they work.
