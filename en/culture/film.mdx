Mawjat's film section covers cinema with a focus on independent, international, and arthouse film — with particular attention to filmmakers from the Arab world and the Francophone world. We review new releases, cover major festivals, and publish in-depth conversations with directors, cinematographers, and producers.

## Festival Coverage

Mawjat covers major international film festivals including the Arab Film Festival, JCC (Journées Cinématographiques de Carthage), DIFF (Dubai International Film Festival), Cannes, Berlinale, and others. Festival reports combine reviews with behind-the-scenes context.

## Arab & Francophone Cinema

While our coverage is international in scope, Mawjat has a special commitment to Arab and Francophone cinema — traditions that are often underrepresented in mainstream English-language film criticism. Our Arabic and French editions carry substantial original coverage of these cinematic traditions.

## Short Films

We are committed to recognising short film as a distinct and important format. Mawjat regularly features reviews and profiles dedicated to short filmmakers.
